🌏 Hong Kong Beyblade Scene

香港爆旋陀螺 — 邊度買、邊度玩、邊度學

🛒 Where to Buy 購買地點

🏪 皆旺商場 (Kwai Wang)

Mong Kok, Sai Yeung Choi St. Multi-floor toy mall with several Beyblade shops. Best for new releases and Takara Tomy originals. Look for dedicated Beyblade stalls on upper floors.

🏪 DC9 動漫集中地

Mong Kok, Sino Centre (信和中心). Multiple shops selling Beyblade products, including rare imports and used Beyblades. Good for finding discontinued parts.

🎯 Toys"R"Us 玩具反斗城

Locations across Hong Kong. Official Takara Tomy distributors. Best for starter packs and new releases. Check their website for stock availability.

📱 Carousell

Hong Kong's largest second-hand marketplace. Search "Beyblade" or "爆旋陀螺" for used Beyblades, rare finds, and collector items. Many sellers offer local meetup.

🛒 Amazon Japan

Best for Takara Tomy exclusives and Japanese releases. Use this link to search with your affiliate support.

🏆 Local Tournaments 本地比賽

Hong Kong has an active Beyblade tournament scene. Tournaments are typically organised through community groups:

  • WBO Hong Kong — World Beyblade Organization sanctioned tournaments. Check their event calendar.
  • Facebook Groups — Search "香港爆旋陀螺" or "HK Beyblade" for local event postings.
  • 皆旺商場 tournaments — Regular weekend tournaments at Kwai Wang. Check with shop owners.
  • Toys"R"Us events — Occasional promotional tournaments during new product launches.

📚 Community Resources 社群資源

💬 Facebook: 香港爆旋陀螺交流區

Active community with 1000+ members. Buy/sell/trade, tournament announcements, and combo discussions.

🌐 WBO (World Beyblade Organization)

The global Beyblade community at worldbeyblade.org. Tournament rankings, forum discussions, and the largest Beyblade database.

📺 YouTube: Beyblade HK

Local content creators who review new Beyblades and cover Hong Kong tournaments.

🎮 Beyblade Formats in Hong Kong

The most popular formats currently played in Hong Kong:

  • Beyblade Burst Standard — Classic Burst format using layers, disks, and drivers.
  • Beyblade X — The newest generation (2023+). Growing popularity in HK.
  • Metal Fight Custom — Niche but active community of Metal Fight collectors.

💡 Tips for HK Collectors

  • Beware of fakes — Always buy from reputable shops or check authentication guides online.
  • Carousell prices — Rare Beyblades are often cheaper on Carousell than retail. Negotiate!
  • Check release dates — Japan gets new Beyblades ~2-3 months before HK retail.
  • Join Facebook groups — Most tournament announcements are made through Facebook, not official channels.
